South African Government to Intensify Crackdown on Online Financial Fraud
National Treasury preparing international cooperation framework to address rising digital scams, Minister Godongwana confirms
Finance Minister Enoch Godongwana is expected to lead South Africa’s upcoming cross-border anti-fraud initiative.

The South African Government has announced a major step toward combating the escalating wave of online financial fraud, signalling the imminent signing of an international cooperation memorandum with several global cybersecurity and financial intelligence partners.

According to senior officials within the National Treasury, the agreement aims to strengthen South Africa’s ability to detect, trace, and dismantle fraudulent investment networks operating across borders — particularly those leveraging deepfake impersonations, cloned trading platforms, and aggressive social-media–based recruitment tactics.
